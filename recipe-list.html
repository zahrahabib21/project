Manto

Manto is a popular Afghan dish consisting of steamed dumplings filled with minced meat (usually beef or lamb), onions, and spices. It's often served with a yogurt-based sauce and garnished with dried mint or red chili. Manto is a flavorful and savory dish enjoyed as a snack or main course, commonly found in Afghan cuisine.

Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a popular Indian and South Asian dessert made from deep-fried dough balls soaked in a sweet syrup flavored with rose water or cardamom. The soft, syrupy texture and rich, sweet taste make it a favorite treat for festivals and special occasions.

Chay

Chay, or tea, is a popular beverage enjoyed worldwide, often made by steeping tea leaves in hot water. In many cultures, especially in Afghanistan, tea is a symbol of hospitality and is commonly served with meals or during gatherings. It can be enjoyed plain or flavored with spices like cardamom, cinnamon, or cloves.
