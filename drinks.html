Mango juice is a delicious, tropical drink made by blending ripe mangoes with water or milk. It's naturally sweet, refreshing, and packed with vitamins, especially vitamin C, making it a perfect thirst quencher on hot days.

Cold Coffee

Iced coffee is a refreshing cold beverage made by brewing coffee and chilling it over ice. It's often sweetened and can be enhanced with milk, cream, or flavored syrups. Popular during warm weather, iced coffee offers a cool and energizing way to enjoy coffee.
