Afghani Manto

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground turmeric (optional)
  • 1 cup plain yogurt (for sauce)
  • 1 tablespoon dried mint (for sauce)
  • 1 clove garlic, minced (optional for sauce)

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, and water to make dough. Knead for 5-7 minutes. Let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the ground meat with onions, cumin, coriander, turmeric, salt, and pepper until browned. Let it cool.
  3. Roll dough into circles and place a spoonful of filling in the center. Seal the edges to form dumplings.
  4. Steam the dumplings for 30-40 minutes until fully cooked.
  5. For the sauce, mix yogurt, dried mint, garlic (optional), and salt. Serve the dumplings with the sauce.

Chay

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 teaspoons tea leaves or 2 tea bags
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Spices (optional: cardamom, ginger, cinnamon)

Instructions

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Add tea leaves and spices. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add milk and sugar, and bring to a boil.
  4. Strain into cups and serve hot.

Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 tablespoons ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1/4 cup milk (or as needed)
  • Oil for frying
  • For the sugar syrup:
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A few drops of rose water

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix milk powder, all-purpose flour, and baking soda.
  2. Add ghee and mix well. Gradually add milk to form a soft dough.
  3. Let the dough rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile, prepare the sugar syrup by boiling sugar and water until the sugar dissolves. Add cardamom powder and rose water.
  5. Heat oil in a pan for frying. Shape the dough into small balls.
  6. Fry the balls on low heat until golden brown. Remove and soak them in the warm sugar syrup.
  7. Let them soak for at least 2 hours before serving.

    Cold Coffee

    • 2 tablespoons instant coffee
    • 2 tablespoons sugar (adjust to taste)

    Malik Cake

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 cup sugar
    • 1 cup butter, softened
    • 4 large eggs
    • 1/2 cup milk
    • 2 teaspoons baking powder
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional)

    Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. In a bowl, cream the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
    3. Add eggs one at a time, beating well after each addition.
    4. Mix in milk and vanilla extract.
    5. Gradually add flour and baking powder. Mix until just combined.
    6. Fold in chopped nuts if using.
    7. Pour the batter into a greased cake pan.
    8. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
    9. Let cool before serving.

    Chocolate Cake

    Ingredients:

    • 1 and 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
    • 3/4 cup cocoa powder
    • 2 cups sugar
    • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
    • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 2 large eggs
    • 1 cup whole milk
    • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
    • 1 cup boiling water

    Instructions

    1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    2. In a large bowl, mix flour, cocoa powder, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
    3. Add eggs, milk, oil, and vanilla. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes.
    4. Stir in boiling water until well combined.
    5. Pour batter into greased cake pans.
    6. Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
    7. Let cool before frosting with your favorite chocolate frosting.

    Afghani Biryani

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups basmati rice
    • 500 grams chicken or lamb, cut into pieces
    • 1 large onion, sliced
    • 2 tomatoes, chopped
    • 1/2 cup yogurt
    • Spices (cumin, coriander, turmeric, and garam masala)
    • Salt to taste
    • Fresh herbs (cilantro and mint) for garnish

    Instructions

    1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
    2. In a pot, sauté onions until golden brown. Add meat and cook until browned.
    3. Add tomatoes, yogurt, and spices. Cook until the meat is tender.
    4. In another pot, boil water and add the soaked rice. Cook until partially done.
    5. Layer the meat mixture and rice, cover, and cook on low heat until fully cooked.
    6. Garnish with fresh herbs and serve hot.

    Pizza

    Ingredients:

    • 2 cups all-purpose flour
    • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 3/4 cup warm water
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 cup pizza sauce
    • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
    • Optional toppings: pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives

    Instructions

    1. In a bowl, combine warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
    2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil to the yeast mixture. Knead until smooth.
    3. Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
    4. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
    5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
    6. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet. Spread pizza sauce over the top.
    7. Sprinkle with mozzarella cheese and add your favorite toppings.
    8. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
    9. Allow to cool slightly before slicing and serving.
